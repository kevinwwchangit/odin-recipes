Spinach and Chicken Casserole


Description

Never had it before, I really don't even like Casseroles


Ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed boneless chicken breast
  • 1 cup Italian salad dressing, or as needed
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1 (8 ounce) package fresh spinach
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup chopped fresh chives
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a bowl and pour in enough Italian salad dressing to coat. Marinate in the refrigerator, 2 to 3 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Drain and discard marinade from chicken and season chicken with garlic powder and lemon pepper. Transfer seasoned chicken to a casserole dish. Layer spinach over chicken.
  3. Mix cream cheese, chives, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Slowly pour chicken broth into cream cheese mixture, stirring until mixture is smooth; pour over chicken and spinach. Cover casserole dish with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove foil and top chicken mixture with mozzarella cheese; cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and cheese is melted, 15 to 20 minutes.
